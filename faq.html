FAQS

Got a question?

Find quick answers below.
If you need more help, contact us.

01 Is MyDigitalGP free to use?

Yes. MyDigitalGP is completely free to use. As an NHS service, it's free at the point of access - just like the NHS care you're already used to.

02 Who can register for MyDigitalGP?

Any patient living within our catchment area who is eligible for NHS care can register.

03 Do I need to leave my current GP?

Yes, registering with MyDigitalGP will transfer your medical records to our practice, becoming your new NHS GP.

04 How do I register?

Simply click the "Register Now" button and follow the quick online registration process.

05 Can I book an appointment now?

Once registered, you can book appointments immediately through our online system.

06 What services are available?

We offer GP consultations, prescriptions, referrals, sick notes, and general medical advice.

07 Is my information secure?

Absolutely. We use NHS-approved secure systems to protect all your personal and medical data.

08 What if I need urgent care?

For medical emergencies, always call 999. For urgent but non-life-threatening issues, call 111.

09 Can I use MyDigitalGP on my phone?

Yes, our platform is fully responsive and works great on smartphones, tablets, and computers.
