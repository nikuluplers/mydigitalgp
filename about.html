ABOUT US

Making NHS care simple, accessible, and digital

MyDigitalGP offers NHS services designed to make accessing healthcare easier. You can manage prescriptions, get medical advice, and book appointments when needed. If an in-person appointment is required, it will be arranged at a location that works best for you.

Our mission

To provide fast, secure, and convenient access to NHS GP services for patients across England – wherever they are.

ABOUT US

Why we exist

Healthcare should fit around your life, not the other way around. Busy schedules and travel barriers make it hard for many people to see a GP when they need to. MyDigitalGP solves this by offering a simple, digital way to connect with NHS care.

Our services

MyDigitalGP makes it simple to get the care you need. Patients can book telephone or video consultations with GPs and pharmacists, ensuring safe, convenient access to advice, prescriptions, and care management.

Looking ahead, we plan to expand our services to include first-contact physiotherapists and mental health practitioners, so you'll be able to access a wider range of healthcare support, all within the same easy-to-use digital service.

Most health concerns can be safely managed without needing to visit the practice, and our clinicians are trained to provide safe, efficient care remotely – making it easier for you to get the right support quickly and conveniently. If you do need to be seen in person, we’ll arrange an appointment at your registered practice location, ensuring you receive the care you need at a location most convenient to you.
